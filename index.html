about me

Photography has been my passion since the early age. I like capturing best moments in my photos. You can see some of my samples in the gallery.

When I was a teenager I joined a photography group called “ The Perspective” from my hometown and a photography group at colleague.

Soon I also infected my family with the passion.

I’m doing family, studio, fashion and outdoor photography.

I believe that in the nearest future I’ll change my passion into profession. At the moment I’m happy to share my passion with the family and friends.
